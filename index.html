Beat Me Up

Instructions

Hello! Welcome to my JavaScript Project! This is a sound visualizer and will analyze sound data like frequency and volume and then display it for you in the canvas. You can either upoad your own music or use an the random sound generator button below to get a random sound from one of the few sound sources provided. Then select the shape of your visualizer, the amount of bars you want in the visualizer, then the size of the visualizer. You can then select whether you want the coloring to be a single color or a rainbow. You can adjust the Hue, Saturation, or the Lightness using the sliders provided below. After all of these settings have been filled in, press the play button in the media control area and watch the visualizer dance!
